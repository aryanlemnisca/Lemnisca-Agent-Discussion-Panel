# Lemnisca Triage — Build-Ready PRD

## 1. Product Name and One-Line Description
**Name:** Lemnisca Triage
**One-Liner:** A low-friction diagnostic calculator that uses bounding physics to instantly eliminate physically impossible root causes during a fermentation deviation.

## 2. Target User
  **Who exactly:** MSAT Process Engineer or MSAT Lead. Technically trained (ChemE/BioE) generalists dealing with multiple concurrent fires; they are not modeling experts.
  **In what situation:** Preparing for an 8:00 AM cross-functional plant standup to frame a deviation investigation, assign resources, and stop Ops from implementing knee-jerk CAPAs.
  **What they have available at that moment:** Sparse data. No aligned historian data, but they have basic batch context and a rough offline OD/biomass reading (e.g., from a 2:00 AM sample).

## 3. Trigger Moment
  **What just happened that makes them open this:** It is 6:30 AM. The night shift operator flagged a deviation at 3:00 AM. The user has 90 minutes to form a defensible technical hypothesis to present to the Plant Manager, QA, and Ops before the historian data is fully extracted and aligned (which takes 48 hours).

## 4. Required Inputs
  1. **Symptom:** Dropdown (Unexplained DO crash/spike; Abnormal Base/Acid consumption; Unexpected OUR/CER divergence).
  2. **Organism Class:** Dropdown (High-density *E. coli*; *S. cerevisiae*; Standard *CHO*).
  3. **Vessel Archetype:** Two Dropdowns — Volume (<2kL, 2-10kL, >10kL) AND Aspect Ratio (Squat <2:1, Standard 2-3:1, Tall >3:1).
  4. **Working Volume:** Numeric input at the time of deviation.
  5. **Phase of Process:** Text/Dropdown (e.g., early exponential, late feed).
  6. **Estimated Biomass:** Numeric input + Unit Dropdown (OD600, Wet Cell Weight, or Dry Cell Weight).
  *Note: Includes a "Transparent Override" toggle allowing users to manually input exact impeller diameter or $k_La$ if known.*

## 5. Processing Logic
  **What the tool computes or structures:** Calculates extreme physical boundaries (Min and Max) for three characteristic time constants: mixing time ($t_{mix}$), mass transfer time ($t_{O2}$), and reaction time ($t_{rxn}$). Computes two dimensionless numbers using interval arithmetic: Macro-Transport Damköhler ($Da_T = t_{O2}/t_{rxn}$) and Meso-Mixing Damköhler ($Da_M = t_{mix}/t_{rxn}$). Routes results through a strict 3x3 Decision Matrix mapping Symptom x Damköhler state.
  **Mathematical or logical basis:** Uses interval arithmetic, stoichiometry, and standard empirical hydrodynamics correlations (e.g., Van't Riet for $k_La$, adjusted circulation time for aspect ratio) to establish physical limits. 
  **What it explicitly CANNOT infer:** It cannot guess or definitively diagnose a specific biological root cause (e.g., it cannot definitively claim "contamination"). It is strictly an engine of elimination rooted in mass transfer and stoichiometry.

## 6. Output
  **Exact format:** A structured "Triage Brief" containing Diagnostic Bounding Statements.
  **What the user receives:** A deterministic conclusion based on the 3x3 Decision Matrix (e.g., "Global mass transfer limitation," "Zonal mixing limitation," or "Physics cleared"). The UI explicitly displays the auto-calculated physical constants (assumed $t_{mix}$ and $t_{O2}$) and a Boundary Sensitivity Warning (e.g., "WARNING: If your actual $k_La$ is greater than 120 hr⁻¹, this conclusion is invalid...").
  **How it is used or shared:** Projected on a screen or read directly to the Plant Manager, QA, and Ops during the 8:00 AM cross-functional standup to bound the 48-hour investigation.

## 7. Trust Mechanism
  **Why an experienced engineer believes this output:** The tool uses transparent "Archetypes with Overrides." It calculates extreme limits rather than single-point estimates, showing its math (assumed constants) directly on the screen.
  **Why it is defensible to QA:** It relies entirely on elimination via physical limits rather than scientifically fraudulent guessing. It utilizes carefully constrained "Diagnostic Bounding Statements" that point to hardware/physics limits or biological divergence, avoiding false-positive "contamination" triggers that would initiate mandatory, costly quarantine cascades.
